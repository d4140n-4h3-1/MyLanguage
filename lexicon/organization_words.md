# Organizational names

## Key

| Suffix | Word    |
|--------|---------|
| -hood  | -reshen |

## Tables

| English     | Word            |
|-------------|-----------------|
| brother     | *freoþir*       |
| brotherhood | *freoþireshen*  |
| sibling     | *sibyaþir*      |
| siblinghood | *sibyaþireshen* |
| sister      | *seoroþir*      |
| sisterhood  | *seorþireshen*  |

I have dropped the "o" in "seorþireshen" to make it feel more natural rather than synthetic. 

Rather than "k"s, I use "þ"s. "K"s are used to indicate kinship.
